Nairobi Summer Coding School

Empowering young minds (8–12) to build, create & innovate

Why Nairobi Summer Coding?

Located in the heart of Nairobi, our program introduces coding through fun, project-based learning. Kids will explore block-based and text-based coding, robotics, game design, and more—all in a safe, supportive environment where creativity comes first.

Our Programs

Block Coding Basics

Learn logic & sequencing with drag-and-drop blocks—no typing required.

Storytelling with Scratch

Create interactive stories, games & animations in MIT Scratch.

Python Adventures

Take your first steps in Python by building simple games & puzzles.

Robotics Explorer

Build and program simple robots using LEGO® WeDo kits.
